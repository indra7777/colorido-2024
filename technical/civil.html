TECHNICAL PAPER PRESENTATION

Description:

This event is aimed to encourage students to review latest literature, prepare a technical report, to make a presentation and to communicate the concept lucidly and to interact with a group of audience to enable them to improve their communication skills, improve their stage presentation and to work with a group.

Rules & Guidelines

  1. This contest is open to all civil engineering students with novel ideas and innovative thoughts.
  2. Students can submit technical papers in any specialization of civil engineering. Each paper can have a maximum of TWO authors.
  3. Papers should be submitted in MS Word document in Times New Roman, 12pt font. The length of the paper is restricted to six A4 size papers.
  4. Author details and Soft copy of Technical paper should be submitted through Google form link on or before 09-02-2024. Selection will be done by the Technical Committee.
  5. Intimation regarding selection of papers will be sent to authors' email on or before 12-02-2024.
Important:

Authors' names, email IDs, college names, and mobile numbers should be printed on the front page of the papers.

TREASURE HUNT

Description:

MODEL MAKING

Description:

Bridge model making competition is to show case the skills in designing aesthetically and economically in the design of bridge. The model will be fabricated by the supplied materials only and tested for strength to weight ratio.

Rules & Guidelines

  1. It is a team event with a minimum of 2 members with an objective of fabricating bridge model of their choice in a fixed time.
  2. Sticks (unlimited), Gum, Cutter and Thread only will be allowed for the fabrication and are provided at the time of event.
  3. A selection criterion is based on Architecture, Dead weight and Maximum load measured at 30 cm span, 11 cm width and length not less than 40 cm.
  4. A maximum of 10 teams will be shortlisted if more teams are registered for the event by a screening test comprises of short answers/multiple choice questions in Solid mechanics, Structural analysis and other general topics in civil engineering.
  5. Duration of model making- 1 hr 30 Min + 10 min Grace
  6. Failure load to the dead weight of the structure is used to assess the efficiency of model.
