Plenary Lecture

Description:

The expert speaker from Industry / Academic Institution will deliver a plenary lecture. The objective of plenary lecture is to explore the recent advances in Chemical Engineering and Allied areas of the field. The session provides a forum for the students to express their ideas and view. Students get benefit to relate theoretical with practical inputs of field. Series of such session pour an extra enthusiasm to student to give practical approach to their study.

Rules & Guidelines

  1. All the participants must be present in the plenary lecture session.
  2. Students are required to maintain perfect silence while lecture is going on.
  3. Students are advised to interact with the expert speaker to clarify your doubts and to express your ideas.

Technical Paper Presentation

Description:

The paper presentation competition provides an opportunity for the students to showcase innovative ideas, learn from fellow enthusiasts, and gain valuable insights from industry/academic experts. It allows participants to enhance presentation and communication skills and update knowledge on latest technologies in chemical and allied engineering.

Rules & Guidelines

  1. All UG and PG students of Chemical Engineering and Allied Courses are eligible.
  2. The authors per paper are restricted to two.
  3. Fill the given google form link given below for registration process.
  4. Technical Paper consists of maximum 08 pages A4 size, Font size 12, Times New Roman in MS Word / pdf format and it should be sent to rvrcheck2024@gmail.com
  5. Presentation time 8 min and 2 min for discussion.
  6. Institution student Identity Card is required.
Important:

Authors' names, email IDs, college names, and mobile numbers should be printed on the front page of the papers.

Elocution

Description:

The art of impactful speech and communication is invaluable in both personal and professional spheres. As budding engineers, developing eloquence to articulate ideas persuasively will be a distinguishing capability as we build our careers. This competition serves as a platform for participants to exhibit their oratory prowess. The objective is to engage in an exchange of ideas, insights, and diverse perspectives through the tool of effective speech.

Rules & Guidelines

  1. Each participant will have a maximum of 10 minutes for their elocution. Exceeding this risks disqualification.
  2. A warning bell will ring at 8 minutes, and the final bell at 10 minutes. Participants must finish their speech by the final bell.
  3. Judges will assess participants based on parameters like content, language, delivery, voice modulation, body language and overall impact. Marks may be deducted for exceeding the time limit.
  4. Our judging panel has been carefully selected for their expertise. They will evaluate speeches fairly based on the assessment criteria.
  5. Participants should focus on articulating their thoughts clearly while engaging the audience. The purpose is to inform, inspire and connect through the powerful medium of eloquent speech.
  6. We request the audience to kindly refrain from disturbances during speeches. Applause is encouraged at the conclusion of each elocution to appreciate the participant.
