    technoFiesta

    Technical Paper Presentation

    Description:

    The paper presentation competition provides an opportunity for the students to showcase innovative ideas, learn from fellow enthusiasts, and gain valuable insights from industry/academic experts. It allows participants to enhance presentation and communication skills and update knowledge on latest technologies in chemical and allied engineering.

    Rules & Guidelines

    1. All UG and PG students of Chemical Engineering and Allied Courses are eligible.
    2. The authors per paper are restricted to two.
    3. Fill the given google form link given below for registration process.
    4. Technical Paper consists of maximum 08 pages A4 size, Font size 12, Times New Roman in MS Word / pdf format and it should be sent to rvrcheck2024@gmail.com
    5. Presentation time 8 min and 2 min for discussion.
    6. Institution student Identity Card is required.
    Important:

    Authors' names, email IDs, college names, and mobile numbers should be printed on the front page of the papers.
